About

This site is a demo application for administration of chemical store. It allows basic operations, such as:

  • Adding the molecules from structure editor or SDF/SMILES files.
  • Searching molecules by their (sub)structure or properties (including SMARTS queries).
  • Purchasing and ordering some amount of molecules.
  • Simple authorization system included.

To try all features, log in with username slavek and password slavek

Remind that it is just a simple demo and we can add every feature you need. We think that our application can be very helpful in every lab that needs some central administration of chemical supplies. And because it is a web application, it can be accessed from every device - PC, tablet, phone. Also it doesn't matter what OS are you using - it will look like same on both Windows and Linux.

If you or your lab is interested in it, feel free to contact us at moldb@chemit.com
